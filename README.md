# StockSense

> **Helping retailers make smarter inventory decisions before problems happen.**

**StockSense is an AI Decision Intelligence Platform for retailers.** It does not merely
report *what happened* — it predicts **what will happen**, explains **why**, and recommends
**what action to take**, with a confidence score and expected business impact attached to
every recommendation.

Traditional software tells retailers "what happened." StockSense tells them what will
happen, why, and what to do about it — before the problem costs them money.

- **The Decision Intelligence Engine is the product.** The AI assistant is only a thin
  window onto it.
- StockSense is **not** an ERP, POS, inventory-management system, CRM, dashboard project,
  or generic AI chatbot.
- Every recommendation is explained, evidence-backed, and confidence-scored — and the
  **human always makes the final decision.**
